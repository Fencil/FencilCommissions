01 — Terms of Service

Average time to complete a commission: 1–3 weeks depending on real life circumstances and complexity of the chart.

This is for 1 chart per commission slot.

Kit only commissions will take up to 1 week to complete per kit, depending on complexity and real life circumstances.

If there are any drum cams, videos, or covers you want me to follow for the song(s) you commissioned, please send them in the ticket.

I will send updates as soon as I have them and tell you how far along I am — when I start working, when I finish, or if something arises that stops me from working. You'll be the first to know.

I'll also send you the song(s) as soon as they're done so you can playtest and tell me what you like and dislike. Any mistakes I will fix before or after completion, for free.

Payments are taken on Ko-fi (supports more payment methods). The payment page will be sent in your commission ticket along with this ToS.
